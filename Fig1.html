Figure 1

This visualization shows max temperature in regard to its latitude. There appears to be a clear uptick at the equator (latitude 0). The variation of the two sides corresponds to the date this was taken, January 5, 2017, which is winter for the Northern Hemisphere and summer for the Southern. It also makes sense that the far ends are the coldest, as those are the closest to the poles.
